# Summary

An about/team page that tells the company story, introduces the humans behind the product, and communicates brand values. Moves from narrative (why we exist) to social proof (who we are) to culture (what we believe). Designed to build trust and humanize the brand.

# Layout & Structure

## Hero

Minimal. Small uppercase label ("About" or "Our Story"). Large headline (48-64px, bold): a concise statement about the company's mission — one powerful sentence. Below: 18px body text (max-width 640px, muted), 2-3 sentences expanding on the mission. No image. Let the words carry it.

## Origin Story

Two-column layout. Left (60%): narrative text blocks. Use 20px body text with comfortable 1.8 line-height for readability. Break into 2-3 paragraphs. Style key phrases in bold or accent color. Right (40%): a single editorial portrait or workspace photo, slightly desaturated, border-radius 12px. The image is vertically centered against the text.

## Metrics Band

Full-width accent section (either solid brand color background or subtle tint). Centered row of 3-4 stats: "5M+ users", "50+ countries", "99.9% uptime", "Series B funded". Each stat: large number (48px, bold, white or dark depending on background), label below (14px, lighter opacity). Separated by thin vertical dividers.

## Team Grid

### Section Header
Centered. "Meet the team" headline (36px). Optional subtext: "The people building the future of [product area]."

### Grid Layout
4-column grid (3-column on tablet, 2 on mobile), 32px gap.

### Team Card
- Photo: aspect-square, border-radius 12px (or full circle for a more personal feel). Object-fit: cover. Desaturated by 15% by default; full color on hover (400ms transition).
- Name: 16px bold, margin-top 16px.
- Title: 14px muted.
- Social links: row of small icons (16px, muted, → accent on hover) — LinkedIn, Twitter/X, personal site. Margin-top 8px.
- Optional hover effect: card lifts slightly (translateY -4px), photo gains full saturation, a subtle background tint appears behind the card.

### Founder Highlight (Optional)
Before the full grid, a larger 2-column layout for founders. Left: large portrait (aspect 3:4, 400px wide). Right: name (28px bold), title (16px, accent), bio paragraph (16px, 3-4 sentences), social links. Thin top border above this section.

## Values Section

### Section Header
Centered. "What we believe" or "Our values" (36px).

### Values Grid
3-column grid, 32px gap. Each value card:
- Icon: 48px, accent color (use simple geometric shapes or Lucide icons).
- Heading: 20px bold. A short value name ("Craft over speed", "Radical transparency", "Users first").
- Body: 15px muted, 2-3 sentences explaining the value. Max-width per card.
- Optional: thin top border in accent color (2px).
- On hover: icon gently rotates 8 degrees, card lifts.

## Culture / Workspace Gallery

Horizontally scrolling image strip (overflow-x: scroll, scroll-snap). 4-6 candid workspace/team photos. Each: 320px wide, 240px tall, border-radius 12px, 16px gap. Slightly desaturated. On hover: full color + subtle scale 1.03.

## Hiring CTA

Full-width section with accent tint background. Centered: "Join us" headline (32px), body text about open roles (18px, max-width 480px), pill button "View open positions →". Simple and direct.
